Sri Lanka is a small island, which is located in Indian Ocean. It takes the shape of a pearl in the world map. Due to the close proximity to the equator, Sri Lanka has a hot and wet climate. Sri Lanka is famous throughout the world as a tourist paradise. There are many tourist attractions in this island. Some of them are the ancient ruins, waterfalls, the botanical gardens, wildlife sanctuaries and sandy beaches.
The ancient ruins can be seen in cities such as Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa, Sigiriya. Sri Lanka has a heritage over 2500 years and so.
The nature wonders such as Dunhinda, Diyaluma and Bambarakanda waterfalls are some of the other attraction in Sri Lanka.
Yala, Kumana, Wilpattu and Wasgamuwa are some of the wildlife parks in Sri Lanka.
Sri Lanka has a long belt of sandy beaches too. Tourists can enjoy themselves swimming, diving and surfing.
The botanical gardens at Peradeniya and Haggala gardens are also attract many tourists.
